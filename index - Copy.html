About Me

Hi, I'm Hugo da Gião, a researcher at HASLab/INESC TEC and Ph.D. student in Informatics Engineering at FEUP. My work focuses on Model-driven DevOps methodologies, creating tools that enable developers to build DevOps pipelines without relying on specific implementations.

My research aims to bridge the gap between complex DevOps practices and developer accessibility, making advanced deployment strategies more approachable through visual and model-driven approaches.

Previous research includes developing visual languages for non-technical users to solve optimization problems, demonstrating my commitment to making complex technical concepts more accessible.
